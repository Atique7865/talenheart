Our Story

Who We Are

TalentHeart Limited is a government-licensed overseas recruitment agency based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We were founded with one mission: to connect hardworking Bangladeshi talent with quality employers around the world.

Since our founding, we have placed over 500 workers in Japan, the Middle East, and Europe — changing lives and supporting families across Bangladesh.

We believe every person deserves a fair shot at a better life. We make the overseas job process transparent, legal, and accessible.
