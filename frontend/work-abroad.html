🇪🇺 Europe

Work in Europe

Europe offers high salaries, strong worker rights, and in many cases a pathway to permanent residency. Germany and Poland have especially high demand for skilled workers from South Asia.

Top Destinations

Germany's Fachkräfteeinwanderungsgesetz (Skilled Worker Immigration Act) opens opportunities for non-EU workers. Jobs in logistics, manufacturing, IT, healthcare.

Salary: €2,200–3,500/month · Benefits: Health insurance, paid leave, pension

Poland is one of the fastest-growing EU economies with high demand for transport, construction and factory workers.

Salary: PLN 4,000–8,500/month · Visa: EU Work Permit

Romania offers EU entry point with lower cost of living. Great for construction, agriculture and factory roles.

Salary: RON 4,000–7,000/month · Visa: EU Work Permit
